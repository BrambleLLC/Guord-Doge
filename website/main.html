The App

Wearable Integration

Using the accelerometer in both your mobile device and your smartwatch, Guard Dog searches for extreme changes in velocity that imply physical trauma. The app will then prompt you to say whether you are alright or not. If you select "NO" or do not respond in time, the app texts your Guard Dogs and calls up to one emergency contact.
